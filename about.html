I'm Tirzah, or 鬲乇夭, or 转执旨专职爪指.

Im a designer passionate about the human-centered, mobility justice side of transportation and the built environment. My background is in illustration and graphic design, but I love learning how to use design in all its forms as a tool to pursue justice. Currently, I'm pursuing a Master of Urban Planning degree from the Harvard University Graduate School of Design.

When Im not thinking about or learning about or practicing design, I can be found drinking black sesame lattes, tending to my ZZ plant Urbosa, learning ASL and Korean, perfecting my technique for folding dumplings, crocheting mittens for my friends, expanding my collection of metro cards from around the world, or attempting to train my cat Professor Kawkaba to fetch.

Want to work with me? I'm already excited. Shoot me an email and let's talk. At the moment, I'm most interested in freelance and part-time opportunities with a focus on community-centered design.
