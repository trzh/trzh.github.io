Changing the game

A justice-centered, climate resilient White Stadium

Planning studio project in Roxbury, MA

Team members: Hannah Jun & Jason Boyle

From 11/2024 to 12/2024

White Stadium is located in Franklin Park, a publicly-owned park in the south of Roxbury, a majority-Black and Latino neighborhood of Boston. The stadium is currently used by Boston Public Schools athletes from around the city, but has historically been a site of disinvestment, speculation, and protest.

In 2023, Mayor Michelle Wu announced plans to renovate White Stadium through a public-private partnership that would see White Stadium leased to a new women's soccer team on key weekends. This plan was met with significant pushback from residents who live near Franklin Park, but the City's plans still move forward.

For our first-semester planning studio's final project, I worked with my teammates to design an alternative proposal for White Stadium's renovation: a value-oriented project that centered the community's needs. At the end of the semester, we presented this project to the Emerald Necklace Conservancy, a key stakeholder advocating for the community affected by this plan.
